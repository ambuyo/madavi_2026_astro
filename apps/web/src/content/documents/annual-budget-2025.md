This budget outlines projected income, operating expenses, and reserve funding for the 2025 fiscal year.

## Key Highlights

- Reserve contributions planned to meet the current reserve study recommendations
- Expected utility, landscaping, and maintenance costs based on vendor contracts
- No special assessment anticipated; assessments remain at the approved monthly rate

## Budget Overview

The 2025 annual budget reflects our commitment to maintaining operational excellence while ensuring adequate reserves for future capital improvements.

### Revenue

Total projected revenue from member assessments and other sources is allocated to cover operating expenses and reserve contributions.

### Operating Expenses

- Administrative costs and management fees
- Utilities and facility maintenance
- Landscaping and grounds keeping
- Insurance and legal expenses
- Staff and contractor salaries
- General operating contingencies

### Reserve Contributions

Reserve funds are being contributed annually to address future capital needs identified in our reserve study.
