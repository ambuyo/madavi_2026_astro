## Annual General Meeting Minutes
**Date:** March 15, 2024  
**Location:** Main Conference Room  
**Attendees:** Board Members, Management, and Shareholders

### Agenda Items

#### 1. Call to Order and Opening Remarks
The meeting was called to order at 10:00 AM. The Board Chair welcomed all attendees and outlined the agenda for the day.

#### 2. Review of 2023 Financial Performance
The Finance Committee presented a comprehensive review of 2023 financial results, including:
- Total revenue exceeded projections by 8%
- Operating expenses were managed within budget
- Reserve fund contributions met targets

#### 3. 2024 Budget Approval
The Board presented the 2024 operating budget for member approval. The budget was unanimously approved with no outstanding objections.

#### 4. Board Elections
The following members were elected to the Board of Directors for a two-year term:
- Director 1
- Director 2
- Director 3

#### 5. Capital Improvements Update
Management provided an update on ongoing capital improvement projects including facility renovations and infrastructure upgrades.

#### 6. Adjournment
The meeting was adjourned at 12:30 PM.

**Minutes prepared by:** Board Secretary  
**Date approved:** April 1, 2024
