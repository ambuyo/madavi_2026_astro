## AI Governance & Risk Management Workshop

A comprehensive 2-day workshop designed for compliance officers, risk managers, and organizational leaders responsible for AI governance.

### Workshop Overview

This hands-on workshop covers the governance structures, policies, and risk management frameworks needed to implement AI responsibly and ensure organizational compliance.

**Dates:** June 5-6, 2026  
**Location:** Kampala, Uganda  
**Format:** In-person (limited virtual seats available)
