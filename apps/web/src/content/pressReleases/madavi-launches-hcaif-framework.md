# Madavi Launches HCAIF Framework for AI Adoption

**Nairobi, Kenya — May 1, 2026** — Madavi Advisory today announced the launch of the Human-Centric AI Implementation Framework (HCAIF), a groundbreaking methodology designed to help organizations navigate AI adoption with focus on people, processes, and governance.

## Overview

The HCAIF Framework addresses a critical gap in the market: while most AI adoption programs focus on technology, they overlook the organizational readiness required for successful implementation. Research shows that 70% of AI pilot projects fail to scale—not because the technology doesn't work, but because organizations weren't prepared to use it.

## Key Components

The framework is built on three foundational pillars:

1. **People Before Platforms** — Assessing human readiness before technology selection
2. **Process Before Product** — Mapping workflows before implementing tools
3. **Governance Before Growth** — Establishing oversight structures before scaling
