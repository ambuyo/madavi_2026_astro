# Madavi Named Top AI Strategy Consultant

**Nairobi, Kenya — March 20, 2026** — Madavi Advisory has been recognized as one of Africa's top AI strategy consulting firms by the African Technology Leadership Council, in their inaugural "Most Innovative AI Adoption Approaches" awards.

## Award Recognition

The recognition highlights Madavi's unique approach to AI adoption, which prioritizes organizational readiness and human-centric implementation over technology-first solutions.

"What sets Madavi apart is their understanding that AI adoption is fundamentally a people and organizational challenge, not just a technology one," said the awards committee. "Their HCAIF Framework represents a paradigm shift in how organizations should approach AI transformation."

## Impact

This award reflects Madavi's:
- 98% client satisfaction rating
- Average 25-40% faster revenue growth for clients implementing AI strategies
- Successful engagements across 15+ African organizations
- Thought leadership in responsible AI adoption

## Founder's Response

"This recognition is gratifying, but the real measure of success is our clients' results," said Madavi Founder & CEO. "When we help organizations build genuine AI readiness—across people, processes, and governance—the outcomes speak for themselves."

## Looking Forward

Madavi plans to expand its advisory capacity and launch the AI Leadership Academy in Q3 2026, a executive development program for C-suite leaders navigating AI transformation.
