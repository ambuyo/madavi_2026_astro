# Madavi Expands Operations Across East Africa

**Nairobi, Kenya — April 15, 2026** — Madavi Advisory announced today the opening of new regional offices in Uganda and Tanzania, expanding its presence across East Africa to meet growing demand for AI strategy and organizational transformation services.

## Growth Momentum

The expansion comes off the back of significant growth in Kenya, where Madavi has successfully delivered AI readiness diagnostics and strategic advisory engagements to 20+ organizations across healthcare, fintech, and professional services sectors.

## New Offices

- **Kampala, Uganda** — Led by Regional Director Akshay Patel
- **Dar es Salaam, Tanzania** — Led by Regional Director Amina Hassan

## Regional Strategy

"East Africa is experiencing a critical inflection point in AI adoption," said Founder & CEO, "Organizations are ready to invest in AI, but they lack the frameworks and guidance to do it responsibly. Our expansion into Uganda and Tanzania positions us to serve this demand while building local expertise and talent."

## About the Expansion

The new offices will provide:
- AI Readiness Diagnostics
- Executive Advisory Retainers
- Leadership AI Fluency Cohorts
- Organizational Transformation Support

For partnership and engagement inquiries in Uganda and Tanzania, contact regional.director@madavi.ai.
