        - Reduced competitive pressure

        - Sustainable competitive advantage

      description: >
        We help you define your unique market position—who you serve, what problems you solve,

  title: Frequently Asked Questions
  description: Answers to questions we hear from prospects considering strategic advisory
  items:
    - question: How is this different from other management consulting?
      answer: >
        Most consulting firms impose pre-built frameworks and solutions on clients. We believe in
        collaborative strategy development—your team remains at the center of decisions, we provide
        expert perspective and facilitate rigorous thinking. We're not selling a methodology; we're
        solving your unique strategic challenge.
    - question: What if we don't implement your recommendations?
      answer: >
        That's entirely your choice. We believe good strategy work provides value even if not fully
        implemented—it clarifies thinking, surfaces options, and helps leadership understand tradeoffs.
        That said, we're committed to developing strategies that your team believes in and wants to execute.
    - question: How much executive time is actually required?
      answer: >
        Realistically, 4-6 hours per week from you and key stakeholders during the 8-12 week
        engagement. This includes interviews, workshops, review sessions, and decision-making. We try
        to batch meetings efficiently, but quality strategy work requires leadership engagement.
    - question: Can you help with implementation?
      answer: >
        Yes. While the core service is strategy development, we can provide implementation support,
        organizational design assistance, or ongoing strategic advisory retainers. Many clients continue
        working with us post-strategy for 6-12 months during implementation.
    - question: How do you measure success?
      answer: >
        We establish success metrics during our discovery phase. Typically these include: clarity of
        strategic direction, leadership alignment on priorities, implementation progress against roadmap
        milestones, and ultimately business outcomes like revenue growth, market share, or profitability
        improvements.
    - question: What if our market changes during the engagement?
      answer: >
        Strategy isn't set in stone. We build flexibility into roadmaps and include quarterly review
        points to recalibrate as market conditions change. Good strategy anticipates change; great
        strategy remains flexible and adaptive.
    - question: How do you price custom engagements?
      answer: >
        We typically scope engagements based on complexity, organization size, breadth of strategy
        (company-wide vs. business unit), and timeline. Most engagements range $35K-$125K. We're happy
        to discuss your specific situation to refine the scope and investment.
    - question: What's your success rate?
      answer: >
        We measure success by client satisfaction and business outcomes. We have a 94% client satisfaction
        rating. More importantly, clients who implement our strategies typically see 20-40% faster revenue
        growth over 18-24 months compared to peers. Success depends on your team's execution, but the
        strategy provides the roadmap.
    - question: Can we start with a smaller engagement?
      answer: >
        Absolutely. We offer 1-day strategy workshops, quick competitive analyses, or focused strategic
        assessments if you want to test working together before a full engagement. Let's discuss your
        situation.
    - question: How do you stay current on strategy and market trends?
      answer: >
        Our team continuously monitors markets, reads research from firms like BCG/McKinsey/Bain,
        maintains relationships with industry experts, attends key conferences, and conducts ongoing
        market research. We bring cutting-edge strategic thinking to every engagement while grounding
        it in practical business reality.
