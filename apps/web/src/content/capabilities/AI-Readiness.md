## The Assessment Process

Our structured approach moves beyond surface-level evaluation to deep understanding of your organization's true AI readiness.

### Phase 1: Discovery
We conduct interviews and workshops across your organization—leadership, operational teams, and affected staff—to gather diverse perspectives on current state and readiness.

### Phase 2: Evaluation
For each of the four pillars, we assess current maturity using our proven framework:
- **Emerging** (1-2): Significant gaps exist, foundational work needed
- **Developing** (3-4): Foundation exists, improvement required
- **Mature** (5): Well-established, functioning effectively

### Phase 3: Gap Analysis
We identify the biggest gaps and prioritize them based on:
- Impact on AI success
- Implementation complexity
- Resource requirements
- Timeline feasibility

### Phase 4: Roadmap Development
We create a phased implementation plan that addresses gaps systematically and realistically, accounting for dependencies and parallel workstreams.

## Addressing Common Gaps

Finding gaps isn't failure—it's the purpose of readiness assessment. Here's how we address them:

**Skills Gaps** → Targeted training programs, strategic hiring, partnerships with external experts

**Data Gaps** → Data collection initiatives, quality improvement programs, governance implementation

**Process Gaps** → Process documentation, redesign engagements, automation opportunities identification

**Cultural Gaps** → Leadership communication strategies, change management programs, early win celebration

## Beyond Assessment

Our engagement doesn't end with a report. We provide:
- Implementation support and advisory
