faqs:
  - category: About Brand Strategy
    questions:
      - question: What is brand strategy and why do I need it?
        answer: Brand strategy is the foundation for how your business positions itself in the market—defining who you serve, what makes you different, and why customers should choose you. Without clear strategy, marketing efforts are inconsistent, messaging confuses prospects, and you compete on price instead of value. Strategy ensures every marketing dollar works toward a consistent, compelling market position.
      - question: How is this different from just hiring a marketing agency?
        answer: Marketing agencies execute tactics (ads, content, campaigns). Brand strategy comes first—it's the foundation that makes tactics effective. Most agencies skip strategy or do surface-level work because tactics generate recurring revenue. We focus on strategy because that's what determines whether your marketing succeeds. Think of it as architecture before construction—you wouldn't build a house without blueprints.
      - question: What's the difference between brand strategy and brand identity (logo, colors)?
        answer: Brand strategy is positioning—who you are, who you serve, what you stand for, how you're different. Brand identity is visual expression of that strategy—logo, colors, typography, imagery. Strategy comes first and informs identity. Many businesses create beautiful identities without strategy, then wonder why marketing doesn't work—visuals can't compensate for unclear positioning.
  - category: Process & Timeline
    questions:
      - question: How long does brand strategy development take?
        answer: Brand Foundation Strategy takes 4 weeks, Market Entry Strategy takes 6 weeks, Brand Audit & Repositioning takes 8 weeks. Timeline depends on engagement type, organization size, stakeholder availability, and research depth needed. We can accelerate for urgent needs (product launch, competitive threat) but recommend minimum 4 weeks for quality research and stakeholder input.
      - question: What information do you need from us to start?
        answer: Existing brand materials (if any), business/marketing plans, customer data and feedback, competitor information, sales materials, previous research, and access to key stakeholders for interviews. Most importantly, we need leadership commitment to participate in discovery interviews and review sessions—strategy fails without leadership buy-in.
      - question: Who needs to be involved from our team?
        answer: Founders/CEO for vision and strategy input, marketing/communications leaders for execution context, sales team for customer insights and competitive intelligence, customer service for customer pain points and feedback patterns. Typically 5-8 stakeholder interviews plus ongoing touchpoints with marketing leadership throughout the engagement.

Plus presentation deck for sharing with stakeholders, messaging guidelines for team use, and training session for marketing and sales teams.
      - question: How do we use the strategy after you deliver it?
        answer: Brand strategy guides every marketing decision—messaging for website and sales materials, content topics and tone, campaign targeting and positioning, visual identity direction, hiring and partner selection. We provide implementation roadmap prioritizing what to update first (usually website, sales decks, core marketing materials) and training to ensure team understands and applies strategy consistently.

        answer: We deliver strategy and implementation roadmap. Implementation (website updates, content creation, campaign execution) typically happens through your internal team or marketing agency. We can provide implementation oversight or connect you with execution partners if needed. Some clients return for quarterly strategy reviews to ensure implementation stays on track.
